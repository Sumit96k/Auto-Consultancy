Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I service my car?

A: It is generally recommended to service your car every 6 months or every 10,000 kilometers, whichever comes first.

Q: How can I improve the fuel efficiency of my car?

A: Some tips to improve fuel efficiency include maintaining proper tire pressure, reducing unnecessary weight in the car, and driving at a consistent speed.
