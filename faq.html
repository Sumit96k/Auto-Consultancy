Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What services does your auto consultancy offer?

A: Our auto consultancy offers a wide range of services, including car buying assistance, vehicle inspections, maintenance advice, performance tuning, and troubleshooting support.

Q: How can your auto consultancy help me with car buying?

A: We provide expert guidance throughout the car buying process. Our consultants assist you in selecting the right car, conducting thorough inspections, negotiating prices, and ensuring you make an informed purchase decision.

Q: Do I really need a vehicle inspection before buying a used car?

A: Absolutely! A vehicle inspection is crucial when buying a used car. Our thorough inspections help uncover any hidden issues, assess the overall condition of the vehicle, and provide you with peace of mind.

Q: How often should I change the oil in my car?

A: The frequency of oil changes depends on various factors, such as the type of oil used and your driving conditions. As a general guideline, it is recommended to change your car's oil every 5,000 to 7,500 miles or as specified in your vehicle's owner's manual.

Q: What are the signs that my car needs a tune-up?

A: Common signs that your car may need a tune-up include rough idling, decreased fuel efficiency, engine misfires, stalling, or a noticeable drop in performance. It's best to consult with our consultants for a proper assessment.

Q: Can you help me with choosing the right tires for my vehicle?

A: Absolutely! We provide tire recommendations based on your specific vehicle, driving habits, and local weather conditions. Our consultants can guide you in selecting the right tire brand, size, and tread pattern for optimal performance and safety.

Q: What should I do if my car's check engine light comes on?

A: If your car's check engine light illuminates, it's important to have it diagnosed by our professionals. The light can indicate various issues, ranging from minor to severe. Ignoring it can potentially lead to more significant problems and costly repairs.

Q: Can you help me with performance upgrades for my car?

A: Yes, we offer performance tuning services to enhance your car's power, handling, and overall performance. Our experts can recommend and install upgrades such as aftermarket exhaust systems, cold air intakes, engine management systems, and suspension enhancements.

Q: How can I improve my car's fuel efficiency?

A: There are several ways to improve fuel efficiency, including regular maintenance, proper tire inflation, smooth driving habits, reducing excess weight, and keeping up with recommended service intervals. Our consultants can provide personalized tips based on your vehicle and driving conditions.

Q: Do you assist with insurance claims after an accident?

A: While we do not directly handle insurance claims, we can guide you through the process and provide the necessary documentation and support to help you with your insurance claim after an accident.

Q: How can I keep my car's interior in good condition?

A: To maintain your car's interior, we recommend regular cleaning, using appropriate products for different surfaces, avoiding prolonged exposure to direct sunlight, using seat covers and floor mats, and addressing any spills or stains promptly.

Q: Can you provide advice on choosing the right car accessories?

A: Absolutely! Whether you're looking for practical accessories like roof racks or performance-enhancing accessories like aftermarket wheels, our consultants can help you choose the right accessories based on your needs, preferences, and vehicle compatibility.

Q: What should I do if my car is experiencing electrical issues?

A: Electrical issues can be complex, and it's best to have them diagnosed and repaired by our experienced professionals. Attempting DIY fixes without proper knowledge can potentially cause further damage. Contact us, and we'll assist you in resolving the electrical issues with your car.
